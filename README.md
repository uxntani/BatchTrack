## 💡 What is this BatchTrack?
**BatchTrack** is a tutor assistant that helps manage batches, students, and their test scores. This app makes it easier for small-scale tutors to keep track of their students progress in a neat format. 

---

## 📊 Features Overview
### ✒️ Teacher side: 
- add batches, 
- add students to specific batches,
- upload notes,
- create MCQ tests,
- view leaderboard,
- assign badges.

### 🎓 Student side is yet to come

## 🚀 Why I built this?

I started tutoring two students about a year ago, and quickly realized that tracking their individual academic progress was quite difficult to manage.

Initially, I used tools like Google Forms to conduct MCQ tests and stored the results in Excel sheets. However, at the end of each month, reviewing performance required going through multiple records manually to identify weak areas. This process was time-consuming and inefficient.

To simplify this, I decided to build a solution for my own use. I began working on this project two months ago, starting with a basic system that could conduct MCQ tests and store results.

Over time, the project evolved into a more complete academic tracking platform. It now includes features such as:
- Creating batches
- Adding and managing students
- Assigning students to specific batches
- Maintaining a leaderboard to track progress
- Uploading notes for future reference

With this system, I can now quickly search for a student and view their performance without manually going through spreadsheets. This has significantly improved both efficiency and clarity in tracking student progress.

## 🚧 Ongoing Development & Future Scope

Currently, the platform is more focused on the teacher side, and further development is required to enhance the student experience.

To support both types of users effectively, the system is being designed with role-based access:

### 👩‍🏫 Teacher Access
Teachers have full control over the platform, including:
- Creating and managing batches
- Adding and organizing students
- Uploading study materials
- Creating MCQ tests
- Viewing leaderboards
- Assigning badges based on performance

### 👨‍🎓 Student Access
Students have limited and focused access, such as:
- Viewing notes and study materials
- Attempting MCQ tests
- Viewing leaderboard rankings

---

## 🔐 Planned Backend Integration
At present, the application uses browser localStorage for data handling. In future versions, it will be upgraded with:
- A proper backend system
- A structured database
- Secure authentication using email

This will allow teachers and students to log in with their own accounts and interact within the same system in a more scalable and reliable way.

---

## 🎓 Upcoming Feature
One planned feature is the generation of a certificate after test completion. Once a student submits a test, the system will generate a downloadable certificate (in .png format) displaying their performance.
